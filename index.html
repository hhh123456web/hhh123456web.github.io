Prota APP

应用介绍

Prota is a mobile application centered on an "AI Journalist" interaction model. Users can simply tell their life stories through voice conversations with AI, and the system will organize, polish, and generate a complete, literary personal autobiography.

Prota 是一款以“AI记者”为核心交互的移动应用。用户无需动笔，只需通过语音与AI对话，讲述自己的人生故事，系统将自动整理并生成一部完整的文学自传。

产品理念

The name "Prota" comes from "Protagonist", meaning everyone is the protagonist of their own life story.

Prota 取自 Protagonist（主角），寓意每个人都是自己人生的主角。
